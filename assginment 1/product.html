What is MeArt?

MeArt is a drawing class that was established by Mr. Nguyen Van Hao at his house. Our main aim is to prepare essential drawing skills for learners who want to enroll in art majors at various universities. However, people who are just simply fond of art or drawing can also enroll in our class, and we will have a different curriculum for this kind of people.
